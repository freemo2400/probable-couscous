Our Mission

At Prilex Steel Detailing we provide our clients with quality and responsive detailing services. It is our belief that the trade of Steel Detailing is at the heart of any steel project, which determines whether a project fails or succeeds despite the quality of the design. We pride ourselves in our ability to recognize the crux of any issue in a project and provide solutions that benefit the steel fabricator while also satisfying the design team. Every project has a unique style and pattern that the architect and engineer communicate through their drawings. We are experienced in recognizing these patterns to boost our efficiency and maximize our production. Prilex Steel will go above and beyond to ensure the success of every project we detail.
